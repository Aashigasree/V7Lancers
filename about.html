About the Company

Innovation at
Our Core

V7Lancers Technopark Pvt Ltd is a technology-driven company based in Tamil Nadu, India, providing innovative software, websites, mobile applications, and digital transformation services for businesses across multiple industries.

We specialize in helping businesses improve productivity, automate operations, and establish a strong digital presence. Our clients span retail, healthcare, education, logistics, and real estate sectors — across India, UK, USA, and Gulf countries.

Our Journey

Timeline &
Milestones

From a passionate freelancing initiative to a full-service technology company — our growth story reflects our commitment to excellence.

Be Part of Our Story →
2016
The Beginning

Started as a small freelancing initiative with 3 founding members, driven by passion for technology and a vision to deliver quality digital solutions.

2018
Private Limited Incorporation

Officially incorporated as V7Lancers Technopark Pvt Ltd in December 2018, marking a major milestone in our journey towards becoming a formal technology enterprise.

2020
Software & Mobile App Expansion

Expanded our service portfolio into customized software development and mobile application development, serving clients across India and internationally.

2022
AI-Powered Products Launch

Introduced proprietary AI-powered products — MeriTrack (online examination) and EezyCRM (project management) — to the Indian market.

2024
250+ Clients & Growing

Reached the milestone of 250+ clients and 450+ completed projects. Launched DocWave, our electronic document management system, and expanded team to 18+ professionals.
